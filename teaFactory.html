Tea Factory Management System (Production Management)

In my 2nd-year, 2nd-semester MERN stack project, I contributed to the development of a Tea Factory Management System, specifically focusing on the Production Management System. My role involved implementing features for managing product stock, including adding new product entries to the dataset, updating existing records, and enabling deletion when necessary. This functionality ensured efficient inventory tracking and streamlined production processes. The project utilized MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, and Node.js, integrating a dynamic and responsive interface for seamless stock management.
